Why It Matters

Private payroll solves a structural trust problem across salary, tax, and access to credit.

Traditional payroll platforms expose too much internally, move too slowly internationally, and still produce weak audit trails. This model moves salary, compliance, and verifiability into one programmable privacy layer.

Traditional payroll

Centralized systems create salary leakage and fragmented trust.

  • HR databases become single points of breach.
  • Cross-border disbursements take days and route through expensive intermediaries.
  • Tax reporting depends on manual reconciliation and opaque spreadsheets.
Private payroll

Encrypted payouts, instant settlement, and proof-driven compliance.

  • Salaries move as shielded stablecoin notes rather than public balances.
  • Employees share threshold-based income proofs without revealing exact compensation.
  • Authorities verify tax and labor obligations with zero-knowledge attestations.

    Architecture

    A privacy-first stack that separates settlement from disclosure.

    Employers fund a shielded pool, payroll logic creates encrypted salary commitments, and a proof engine emits audience-specific attestations for employees, banks, and regulators.

    01

    Company treasury

    Corporate wallets deposit USDC, DAI, or PYUSD into a payroll vault with scheduled rules.

    02

    Privacy layer

    Aztec-style shield contracts and Noir circuits convert payroll instructions into encrypted notes.

    03

    Proof engine

    Tax, income, minimum-wage, and audit proofs are derived without exposing raw salary history.

    04

    Disclosure portals

    Employees, auditors, and tax authorities each receive only the statement they are authorized to verify.

    Privacy L2

    Aztec Network / private zk-rollup

    Shielded transfers, private state, and Noir-compatible circuit execution.

    Contracts

    Noir + Solidity orchestration

    Payroll schedules, recovery policies, and treasury permissions sit in audited contracts.

    Identity

    Wallet + employee credential

    ENS, soulbound employment credentials, and optional KYC tokens identify legitimate recipients.

    Data

    Encrypted payslips + oracle feeds

    IPFS or secure storage retains payslips while Chainlink-style feeds power FX and tax calculations.
